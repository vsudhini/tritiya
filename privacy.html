Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Tritiya ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information from website visitors and campaign participants, including SMS messaging participants.

Data Collection

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and message content submitted via forms.
  • Messaging data: SMS opt-in status, HELP/STOP requests, and related message interaction metadata.
  • Technical data: browser type, device type, IP address, and basic analytics events.

Data Usage

  • To respond to inquiries and provide our services.
  • To manage messaging programs (including opt-in/opt-out), and to send service-related SMS updates.
  • For internal analytics, performance monitoring, and compliance with carrier/campaign requirements.

Data Sharing

  • Service providers processing data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Carriers and registration partners as required to provision and maintain messaging campaigns.
  • Legal disclosures when required by law, regulation, or to protect our rights.

Mobile SMS Privacy

Required statement:

"Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties."

Opt-Out

If you wish to be removed from receiving future communications, you can opt out by texting STOP or QUIT at any time. For assistance, reply HELP.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at itstritiya@gmail.com.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at itstritiya@gmail.com.
